Destiny 2

Destiny 2 is a multiplayer looting first person shooter. Its very unique from other shooters where it has a big lore and story. Where you "the guardian" needs to complete missions and tasks in order to level up your power "light". And obtain amazing and fun weapons. Destiny can be played just of its story and in PvE or PvP against other players.

In destiny you get to chose one of 3 classes. Hunter, Titan or Warlock which all promotes diffrent kind of gameplay and abillities.And each class also have their own subclasses or elements such as Void Solar Arc or Stasis. Which all also promotes diffrent playstyles and can all be used in their own way to defeat players or bosses.

Its a very complex but very fun game but it can take some time before you learn it and if you have friends who play it its a good idea to take their help in your first missions and quests.
