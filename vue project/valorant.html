Valorant

Valorant is a online based multiplayer shooter. Its one of the most popular shooter games available and enjoyable aswell. It features unique maps and unique characters with unique abillities to attack / defend easier. Such as smokes grenades flashbangs and much more. Its also economy based like its close relative counterstrike. This means that you need to win / stratigies your economy to afford the more better guns that you will want.

You can play Valorant for fun with you friends or try diffrent stratigies your way on the rankings and compete at a higher stake and there is always a agent for you!

For its free price and low amount of cheaters and amount of fun there is no other tactical shooter game i could recommend higher over this 9/10.
